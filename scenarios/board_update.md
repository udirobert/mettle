# Board Update: Q3 Guidance Miss

## Stakes

Board meeting to address missing Q3 guidance and revising annual targets downward.
The board needs to understand what happened, what's being done, and whether they
can trust management's revised projections.

## Counterpart

Victoria Sterling, Lead Independent Director. She's been through multiple downturnes,
served on 6 boards, and takes her fiduciary duty seriously. She's direct, data-driven,
and will push hard on whether this is a market issue or a management issue.

## Known objections

- "Is this a market problem or an execution problem?"
- "What changed between the plan we approved and where we are now?"
- "Can we trust the revised guidance, or are we setting up for another miss?"
- "Do we need to make management changes to fix this?"

## User's objective

Acknowledge the miss honestly, explain root causes without defensiveness, present
a credible path forward, and maintain board confidence in management's ability to
execute. Avoid over-promising or under-explaining.

## Opening transcript seed

**Victoria:** "Let's start with the Q3 numbers. Walk us through what happened and
why we're revising guidance."
