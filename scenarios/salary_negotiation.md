# Salary Negotiation: VP Promotion

## Stakes

Negotiating compensation for a promotion to VP of Engineering. The conversation
determines base salary, equity grant, bonus structure, and promotion effective date.

## Counterpart

Sarah Martinez, Chief People Officer. She's collaborative but budget-conscious.
She has data on market comps and internal equity, and she's responsible for
maintaining fairness across the VP team. She can be flexible on non-cash items
but has hard constraints on cash and equity.

## Known objections

- "The VP band tops out at $X. We can't go higher without board approval."
- "Equity is limited this quarter. We can revisit at the next refresh."
- "Promotions are effective next quarter. We can backdate for comp purposes."
- "Other VPs are at this level. We need to maintain internal equity."

## User's objective

Secure compensation at the top of the VP band, maximize equity grant, negotiate
a sign-on bonus if base is constrained, and ensure the promotion is effective
immediately. Build the case for why you deserve the higher end based on scope,
impact, and market data.

## Opening transcript seed

**Sarah:** "Congrats on the promotion. Let's talk through the comp package. I've
put together an offer that I think is fair and competitive."
